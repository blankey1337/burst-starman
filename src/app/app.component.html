What is Burst?

Burst is a cryptocurrency from the future.

Using a revolutionary consensus protocol called Proof of Capacity, Burst practically eliminates the energy costs associated with mining.

  • 5000X more energy efficient than Bitcoin1
  • Truly decentralized: no pre-mine, no ICO
  • Smart Contracts, Asset Exchange, and more

Wait, seriously?

To celebrate the launch of the new Phoenix Burst Wallet, we wanted to do something that symbolizes our excitement and celebrates our achievement. This is why we’ve linked up with the crazy minds at StackBlitz to launch this website promoting Burst into space.

Why are we doing this?

We believe the new Phoenix Burst Wallet makes it so easy to send and receive Burst, that even an Alien could do it. Also, life is short, and we think it’s important to work on things that inspire and motivate us. There’s just something about sending our software into space that appeals to us.
